Project Overview

The Problem:

The problem is not knowing the location and estimated time of arrival of the attendees of the event without having to constantly message them for updates.

The Solution:

The solution is an effortless and straightforward system that allows you to see the current location and estimated time of arrival of the attendees as a result of them instantly sharing their location as they sign up for the app.

Tools Used:

Sketch and inVision (Prototyping), Paper and Pencil (Wireframing)

Discovery

User Interviews

Before even diving into the features and the overall UX of my product I conducted some user interviews so I can gain a better understanding of the problem. These are some of the key takeaways from those interviews:

Key Insights

  1. Locating all of the attendees before an event starts takes way to much time and can be exceedingly frustrating.
  2. Organization is alway key to planning events. When planning an event, it is essential that you keep all of the information organized and clear.
  3. Planning events with a large amount of attendees is both stressful and time consuming.

Key Experience Principles

  1. Process: The process of having to message all of the attendees just to be able to see how close they are is stressful and takes too much time. Being able to see someones location and estimated time of arrival just by a touch of a finger is less time consuming and takes away the pain of having to get in touch with everyone.
  2. Location: Being able to have your friends immediately share their location as they sign up for the app allows you to locate them when you want to know how close they are to the event.
  3. Accuracy: Being able to have everyone share their location, not only helps you to see how far away the attendees but allows you to get their accurate estimated time of arrival.

Persona

Meredeth - Primary

Meredith G.

  • 21 years old
  • Gender: Female
  • 3rd year college student
  • Lives in an apartment on campus
  • Has 2 roomates
  • Waitress at a local restaurant

“I just want an easier way to make plans with my friends.”

Meredeth is a 21 year old college student who lives in an apartment with two of her close friends. She is an extrovert who loves to talk and enjoys having people around her. Meredeth is constantly hanging out with her roomates and her friends. She is with her friends to either study, grab some food or to meet up for the night. Sometimes it is hard for her to invite all of her friends at once and trying to get them to the same place. She also wants to be able to know who is all coming and when time comes how close her friends are to the event that was planned.

Meredith's Goals:

  • Wants to be able to create events easily
  • Wants an simple way to invite all of her friends to events
  • Wants to be able to see the location of her friends to see how close they are to the destination

Technical Background:

  • Owns an iPhone and a laptop
  • Frequently uses messages, email and camera
  • Frequently uses social media apps like Instagram, Twitter, Snapchat and Facebook
