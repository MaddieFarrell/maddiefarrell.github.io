About me

Welcome to my portfolio!

I'm Madeline Farrell! I am a designer who is extremely passionate about designing phenomenal graphic designs, user interfaces, and user experiences. I am currently a student at Arizona State University majoring in Graphic Information Technology. Prior to enrolling in the Graphic Information Technology program at ASU, I was a self-taught designer, ultimately acquiring experience in graphic and UX design through MOOCs, workshops, personal projects, and freelancing. As a designer, I always mock, prototype, and iterate on my designs with the users' needs in mind and at the core of the design. I am passionate about designing great and impactful products!
