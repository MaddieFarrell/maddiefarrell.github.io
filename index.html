About me

Welcome to my portfolio!

I'm Madeline Farrell, a user experience designer who is passionate about designing phenomenal UX. I will receive my B.S. in Graphic Information Technology from Arizona State University in 2020. I started my journey into UX through independent study of MOOCs, workshops, personal projects, and freelancing. My happy place is when I’m prototyping and designing iteratively in organization with a user-centric focus.

Besides that, I enjoy eating Korean bbq and ice cream, and I love to travel. I have a small coffee addiction, and I illustrate Disney characters whenever I have some free time.
